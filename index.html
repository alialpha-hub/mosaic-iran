Ethnic & Cultural Profiles

Persians

The primary ethnic group of Iran, descendants of the ancient Elamite and Aryan tribes. They are predominantly Shia Muslims and speak Farsi.

Azerbaijanis

A Turkic-speaking group with a massive cultural footprint in Northwest Iran. Known for their incredible bazaars and literary history.

Kurds

Living primarily in the Zagros Mountains. Their culture is defined by unique folk dances, mountainous lifestyle, and their own distinct language.
